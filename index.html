About Us

In industries like real estate, encountering the problem of high attrition rate is a common thing, same way as it is critical to explore ways to ensure employee retention. An approach that surely relieves this situation is by developing positive work relationships. Not most of the company accord the same importance in this concern but some company has been emphasizing this and they truly deserve a spotlight.

JCA Business Support Services provides a workplace that is beyond practices to turn the company into a family. It shows that at the heart of every great workplace is a caring relationship between people. This place represents a great home for everyone. There is no discrimination and we are guaranteed equal opportunities for everyone regardless of what your situation is, as long as you are able to communicate effectively. This factor is substantial as it influences quality performance, reduces the job-related stress, increases job satisfaction, promotes higher productivity, reduces absenteeism and establishes higher ownership of customer concerns. Therefore, we approach work with energy, enthusiasm and increased willingness.

Aside from these, the company’s overall performance as a team absolutely satisfies the clients. This is one of the competitive advantages of JCA among other lined companies in the industry. People here are heartily carrying out their jobs and bringing the best quality service.

What we offer

Cold Calling

Our way of lead generation for Real Estate or finding possible property seller for our clients. In this process, we call every single person we can in our client’s preferred location or area in which they want to invest or purchase a property.

Inbound Calling

Is one that a customer initiates. This can be an inquiry from a customer, a possible lead, or a call back from the cold callers’ calls. Inbound call can be powerful because the caller is actively getting into contact with your company, results into sales.

SkipTracing

A method used to search and verify a person’s demographics. The process involves using tools and sites to gather and validate as much data as we can on a person. We can collect, analyze and verify information to determine the location of an individual.

Web Development

Web development is the multidisciplinary process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications for the internet or an intranet. It encompasses a wide range of tasks, including designing the user interface, structure and performance of websites.
